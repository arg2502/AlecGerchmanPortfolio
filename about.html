Alec R. Gerchman

Hello, my name is Alec Gerchman.

I am currently a fourth year student at Rochester Institute of Technology pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Game Design and Development as well as a Music and Technology minor. I was born and raised in the small town of Dunmore, Pennsylvania. I graduated from Dunmore High School in 2013 ranked fourth in my class.

Whenever I have free time(and whenever I'm not playing video games...) I like to spend my time honing my skills and just being creative. My go-to is writing music. I have a couple works up on SoundCloud, but I have not yet produced many high quality pieces yet. I also like writing up aspects of fictional worlds to practice creative writing. When I'm not doing anything artsy, I turn to the programming world and discover new techniques in the coding world.

I have been fascinated with all types of games for most of my life, but most of all, I have also loved video games. I believe video games are the perfect medium to express creative ideas. They require interaction and make the player be part of a story, be part of a different world. And games are not always just get from point A to point B. There's always an atmosphere to great games with the visuals, the sounds, the immersion into the world, and so many other elements. It's about introducing people to a world you have only ever imagined. That is why I love game design and development.
