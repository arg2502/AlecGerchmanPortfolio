Allons-y to the Future

An Original Musical by Zack Peercy and Alec Gerchman

An epic journey across time, space and fandoms!

Allons-y To The Future is an original parody musical combining the worlds of Back to the Future and Doctor Who.

This original musical was produced by Zack Peercy and Alec Gerchman in Dunmore, Pennsylvania during the summer of 2014. Alec was responsible for all musical aspects of the show, including the composition of all of the show's original tracks, while Zack was responsible for the script. In addition to portraying the lead role of the Doctor in the production, Alec also assisted with fundraising efforts for the show and served as co-director.

This parody musical was recorded and presented free to the public at the Dunmore High school auditorium. The musical was produced and performed solely for the personal, non-commercial enjoyment of the cast and crew as well as other Doctor Who and Back to the Future enthusiasts. It is in no way sponsored, approved, endorsed by or affiliated with Universal Studios, BBC or any of either party's affiliates.
