Coursework

Please refer to my resume for full list of completed courses.

Game Design & Dev. 1 and 2

This course examines the design and development paradigm from story-boarding and pre-visualization through refinement and structured prototyping exercises to examine the validity of a design.

Students examine techniques for managing innovation and creativity in a game development-specific context, and for world and character design.

3D Animation and Asset Production

This course provides an overview of 3D game asset production.

Topics covered include modeling, texturing, skinning and animation. Emphasis is put on low polygon modeling techniques, best practices in game art production, and effective communication strategies between artists, programmers and designers.

Data Structures & Algorithms 1 and 2

This course focuses upon the application of data structures, algorithms, and fundamental Newtonian physics to the development of video game applications, entertainment software titles and simulations.

Topics covered include quaternion representation of orientation and displacement, cubic curves and surfaces, geometric primitives and classifiers.

Game Dev. & Algorithmic Problem Solving

In this course, students will learn elements of game software development, including problem decomposition, the design and implementation of game applications, and the testing and debugging of their designs.

Students are also introduced to data structures, algorithms, exception handling and design patterns that are relevant to the construction of game systems.
